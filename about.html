Chairman of the Board

Kolapo Omidire is an experienced Management Consultant with over 25 years experience. He is passionate about the successful operation and expansion of high growth companies, and has served as the Chairman of our board of Directors since ....... From 1996 to 2009, Mr. Omidire held multiple positions at Guaranty Trust Bank, including legal advisor for over 10 years. He was also the founding Managing Director of Guaranty Trust Assurance, now known as Axa Mansard and GT Homes, now known as Imperial Homes. Prior to joining GTB, Mr. Omidire was the company secretary of Dunlop Nigeria PLC, and DN Meyer. Mr. Omidire was selected to serve as a chairman on our Board due to his broad industry experience and extensive legal and corporate leadership experience.
