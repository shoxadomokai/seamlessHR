End-to-end HR

SeamlessHR is an all-in-one software built with a full focus on HR. It's like an ERP for HR.

Gusto works best if you're a small-sized business with US-only presence and you have a thing for expensive software.

Ease of use

SeamlessHR is simple, intuitive; delights employees and makes HR look forward to their next tasks.

Gusto is easy to use, but the price tag it comes with makes you feel like you lost more than you benefited.

Payroll localisation

SeamlessHR takes a Pan-African approach towards Payroll. Already localised to many African countries. Also includes in-bult tools for you to customise your Payroll module to fit your company.

"Question: ""[Gusto,] Do you offer international payroll?"" Gusto: ""No, we can only process payroll for employees in the US."" https://gusto.com/product/faq"

Customisation

Customisation is at the very core of how we build at SeamlessHR. SeamlessHR comes with in-built user-friendly tools but if you find that you still have unique needs, you can always talk to us to discuss a customisation.

Customisations aren't available on Gusto. You have to adjust your HR to fit the software.

Service

SeamlessHR offers simple, context-driven, heart-to-heart support directly to the users on your HR team. It's the kind of support that takes the words right out of your mouth.

Gusto is already expensive for most businesses. But then you need another subscription fee to access HR-context support.

Pricing

We believe every business should be able to afford good HR software. Transparent, no-surprises pricing that lets you manage your costs as you grow.

Gusto can cost you up over $100,000 per year plus add-ons if you have 200 staff.

Setup experience

SeamlessHR has an intuitive self sign-up experience to help you get things rolling. You can also request help from the Onboarding team if you need further assistance.

Gusto has a good setup experience. But for what it costs, anyone would expect more.

Integrations

SeamlessHR has a growing library of integrations to help your employees get more productive ranging from Calendars to bank account payments via Payroll.

Gusto has a marketplace of integrations and apps but they're literally additional straws into your business's pockets.
