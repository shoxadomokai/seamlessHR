JOB MANAGEMENT

Manage your job openings.

Create job openings and share across the web with a unique URL. Job openings are synced with your organogram in real-time. LinkedIn integration coming soon.

APPLICANT TRACKING

Track, search, sort, filter, download - everything.

Collect an unlimited number of applicant CVs, and search through them using keywords. Or automatically group candidates based on criteria you set. Then move candidates through custom workflows, and download their dossiers if you want. Applicants also get their Self-Service login to track their applications.

COLLABORATIVE HIRING

Hire with your team.

Share role-based access to hiring managers and other recruitment stakeholders. Get their feedback in shortlisting or interviewing candidates.

INTERNAL RECRuITMENT

Keep your brightest minds in roles that interest them.

Internal recruitment lets you mark a job as open to current employees, and allows them apply with simple clicks. Their applications are pre-populated with already available data from CoreHR.

Talent Pool

Never lose contact with a candidate again.

All your job applications go into your talent pool. Search and match candidates to jobs at any time. Also, notify candidates when a job opening matches their interests, if you want.

Competency-based Interviews

Custom interview notes for all your roles.

Create custom interview notes to facilitate competency-based assessments. Works like magic when used with Collaborative Hiring.
