End-to-end HR

SeamlessHR is an all-in-one software built with a full focus on HR. It's like an ERP for HR.

Gusto works best if you're a small-sized business with US-only presence and you have a thing for expensive software.

Sage 300 is a good Accounting & Payroll software masking as a HR software.

Zenefits works best if you're a small-sized business with US-only presence and you have a thing for expensive software.

SAP has a good ERP with generally weaker HR modules. Even with the acquisition of SuccessFactors, SAP still doesn't have the best end-to-end HR sofware.

Oracle has a good ERP with generally weaker HR modules. Even with the acquisition of PeopleSoft, SAP still doesn't have the best end-to-end HR sofware.

Ease of use

SeamlessHR is simple, intuitive; delights employees and makes HR look forward to their next tasks.

Gusto is easy to use, but the price tag it comes with makes you feel like you lost more than you benefited.

Sage 300 's user experience is not intuitive enough. Your employees will still need some guiding around.

Zenefits is easy to use, but the price tag it comes with makes you feel like you lost more than you benefited.

SAP SuccessFactors has an interphase that most of your millenial workforce may not readily connect with.

Oracle PeopleSoft has an interphase that most of your millenial workforce may not readily connect with.

Payroll localisation

SeamlessHR takes a Pan-African approach towards Payroll. Already localised to many African countries. Also includes in-bult tools for you to customise your Payroll module to fit your company.

"Question: ""[Gusto,] Do you offer international payroll?"" Gusto: ""No, we can only process payroll for employees in the US."" https://gusto.com/product/faq

Sage 300 is good at Payroll generally, but hasn't really been able to crack localisation for multiple countries. Many clients battle with different issues.

"""Zenefits support[s] the ability to hire international workers, but does not support the ability to pay them."" - Zenefits. https://help.zenefits.com/Hiring/How_to_hire_in_Zenefits/Hiring_and_Paying_International_Workers/"

On SAP SuccessFactors, It will cost you a lot of implementation manhours and money to get a localised Payroll, and even more to get it customised to fit your company.

On Oracle PeopleSoft, It will cost you a lot of implementation manhours and money to get a localised Payroll, and even more to get it customised to fit your company.

Customisation

Customisation is at the very core of how we build at SeamlessHR. SeamlessHR comes with in-built user-friendly tools but if you find that you still have unique needs, you can always talk to us to discuss a customisation.

Customisations aren't available on Gusto. You have to adjust your HR to fit the software.

Sage 300 isn't flexible enough to accomodate much of your customisations. For clients who insist, the experience often gets messy.

Customisations aren't available on Zenefits. You have to adjust your HR to fit the software.

On paper, customisations are available with SAP SuccessFactors. Only that they would probably cost you a few millions of dollars.

On paper, customisations are available with Oracle PeopleSoft. Only that they would probably cost you a few millions of dollars.

Service

SeamlessHR offers simple, context-driven, heart-to-heart support directly to the users on your HR team. It's the kind of support that takes the words right out of your mouth.

Gusto is already expensive for most businesses. But then you need another subscription fee to access HR-context support.

Sage 300 offers regular, typical, generic kind of support.

Zenefits is already expensive for most businesses. But then you need another subscription fee to access HR-context support.

SAP's Support experience can be intimidating to HR users who are not able to grasp all the technical jargon.

Oracle's Support experience can be intimidating to HR users who are not able to grasp all the technical jargon.

Pricing

We believe every business should be able to afford good HR software. Transparent, no-surprises pricing that lets you manage your costs as you grow.

Gusto can cost you up over $100,000 per year plus add-ons if you have 200 staff.

Sage 300's Admin licenses cost thousands of dollars - for just Payroll. You have to shell out more money if you're adding the HR modules. And of course you pay for the upgrades.

Zenefits can cost you up to $80,000 per year plus add-ons if you have 200 staff.

SAP Pricing can carry lots of surprises. There are lot of third-party costs including those for implementation that could potentially run you out of a business.

Oracle Pricing can carry lots of surprises. There are lot of third-party costs including those for implementation that could potentially run you out of a business.

Setup experience

SeamlessHR has an intuitive self sign-up experience to help you get things rolling. You can also request help from the Onboarding team if you need further assistance.

Gusto has a good setup experience. But for what it costs, anyone would expect more.

Setting up on Sage 300 can be difficult. You need an external implementation vendor, costs exclusive of your original bill.

Zenefits has a good setup experience. But for what it costs, anyone would expect more.

Setting up SAP can be difficult. You will need to go through endless documentation and give up thousands of company manhours.

Setting up Oracle can be difficult. You will need to go through endless documentation and give up thousands of company manhours.

Integrations

SeamlessHR has a growing library of integrations to help your employees get more productive ranging from Calendars to bank account payments via Payroll.

Gusto has a marketplace of integrations and apps but they're literally additional straws into your business's pockets.

Sage 300 has a limited integration library. The integrations don't help your employees get more productive and you have to hire one of their partners to get them.

Zenefits has a marketplace of integrations and apps but they're literally additional straws into your business's pockets.

SAP has a large integrations library. But things get complicated often. You need a lot of resources to make your integration dreams come true.

Oracle has a large integrations library. But things get complicated often. You need a lot of resources to make your integration dreams come true.
